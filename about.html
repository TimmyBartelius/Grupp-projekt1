At Pear, we are transforming the world of virtual reality with cutting-edge VR headsets designed to bring digital worlds to life.

Pear was born in a small garage, founded by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts who shared one goal: to create a new standard in virtual reality. Frustrated by the limits of existing technology, we began our journey by blending our skills in software, hardware, and design to develop a product that was both powerful and user-friendly.

Our mission is simple: to make high-quality virtual reality experiences accessible to everyone. From immersive gaming and professional simulations to groundbreaking educational tools, Pear’s VR headsets push the boundaries of what's possible.
